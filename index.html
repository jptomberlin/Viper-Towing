About

Viper Towing & Recovery, LLC was founded in 2012 by Mark & Rachel. The business was started to offer an affordable alternative to high priced towing companies.

While serving as a well respected Police Officer for the City of Colorado Springs, Mark received numerous commendations and awards because of his outstanding work ethic and investigative skills. Rachel has successfully owned and operated a small auto detailing business and utilitzes her organizational and customer service skills to help run their successful company.

At Viper Towing & Recovery, LLC we are happy to serve Colorado Springs and the surrounding areas for any towing needs.
